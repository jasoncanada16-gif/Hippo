FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does EV charger installation cost in Ontario?

Most EV charger installations range from $500 to $2,000 depending on wiring distance, electrical panel capacity, charger type and installation complexity.

Do I need a panel upgrade for an EV charger?

Not always. A licensed electrician can perform a load calculation to determine whether your existing electrical service can safely support a Level 2 EV charger.

How much does a 200 amp panel upgrade cost?

A typical 200 amp panel upgrade in Ontario ranges from $2,500 to $5,500 depending on the existing service, utility requirements and equipment needed.

How much does pot light installation cost?

Pot light installation typically costs between $150 and $250 per light depending on ceiling accessibility, wiring requirements and the type of fixtures selected.

How long does a panel upgrade take?

Most residential panel upgrades can be completed within one day, including coordination with the utility company and ESA inspection requirements.
